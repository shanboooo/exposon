3 个硬件，对应 3 类外场任务 Three devices for three field tasks

Exposon Thm 面向热暴露，Exposon Cam 面向街景记录与语义分割，Exposon Air 面向空气暴露监测。所有配置按同一方式排布，便于直接阅读。 Exposon Thm is built for thermal exposure, Exposon Cam for street capture and semantic segmentation, and Exposon Air for air-exposure monitoring.
